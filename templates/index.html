Welcome to the Web Page to find recommendations of Covid-19 Research Articles

The objective of this web page is to help researcher to find similar research papers of ones interest. The web application reccommends relevant COVID-19 research articles for a given query article. The database for the recommendations are based on scholarly research articles of the COVID-19 Open Research Dataset (CORD-19), details is available in Kaggle.

To access the recommended articles go to the 'Input' link and submit a paper_id of an article
